This garph shows the weighted network of coappearances of characters in Victor Hugo's novel "Les Miserables".

Nodes represent characters as indicated by the labels and edges connect any pair of characters that appear in the same chapter of the book. The values on the edges are the number of such coappearances. The data on coappearances were taken from D. E. Knuth, The Stanford GraphBase: A Platform for Combinatorial Computing, Addison-Wesley, Reading, MA (1993).
